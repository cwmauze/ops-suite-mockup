Ops Suite Prototype

v0.5.1
⚠️ For Metro Aviation Internal Testing Only

This is a semi-functional prototype designed to experiment with and implement user feedback. Its primary goal is to prototype a new, optimized user interface for the app previously known as Complete Flight.

✨ Key Improvements

  • Single-Screen Layout: All critical flight information fits on a single iPad screen without any vertical scrolling.
  • Offline Obstacle Clearance: Automatically calculates the highest obstacles along your flight path, even without an internet connection, and lets you quickly toggle between the Route Highest Obstacle or the Leg MSA.
  • Aviation Maps & ForeFlight Sync: Integrated full-screen maps with FAA Charts and live weather overlays. Routes are now sent directly to ForeFlight using official FAA identifiers, not just coordinates.
  • Pre-Populated Risk Assessment: Streamlined risk assessment that automatically pre-loads known risk factors for quick pilot review and acknowledgment.
  • Revamped Flight Log: Fully redesigned manifest page featuring quick drag-and-drop crew seating and rapid onload/offload capabilities, eliminating the need to search for personnel every time.

📱 Install on iOS (PWA)

For the most realistic experience, install this prototype as a native app on your iPad or iPhone:

  1. Open this page in Safari on your device.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow pointing up).
  3. Scroll down and select Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top right corner.
  5. Go to your Home Screen and open the new Ops Suite app to experience the UI in full-screen native mode.
